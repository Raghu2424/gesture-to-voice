About the Project

The Communication Challenge

Speech-impaired individuals face significant barriers in daily communication. Traditional methods like sign language are not universally understood, and typing is slow and cumbersome.

Our Solution: A real-time gesture recognition system that converts hand gestures into clear, natural speech using cutting-edge computer vision and AI.
